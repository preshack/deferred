The Disconnect Phase (Turn Wi-Fi OFF!)

Crucial Stage Theatrics: Physically turn off your Mac's Wi-Fi right now in front of the judges! Prove there is absolutely zero internet connection.

A. Bob Scans the QR Code

python3 bob_scan_qr.py
  • The script will prompt you: Enter exactly how much to charge Alice (e.g. 5.00): $
  • Type: 5.00 and hit Enter.
  • Your webcam will turn on. Point it at Alice's QR code on your screen!
  • What happens behind the scenes: Alice's offline balance was pre-deducted at QR generation. Bob's settlement credits his wallet with net_cents (amount minus 3% processing fee). Any unused token value is automatically refunded back to Alice's offline pool — she only loses what was actually charged.

B. The Double Spend Defense (Still Offline)
